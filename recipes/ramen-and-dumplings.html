Ramen and Dumplings

Description

A warm and cheap dish that brings all the umami to the table.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet flavored ramen of your choice.
  • 1 package frozen dumplings of your choice.

Steps

  1. Set aside ramen seasoning packet and dumpling sauce packet if applicable. Cook dumplings and ramen per package instructions.
  2. Empty ramen seasoning packet into a bowl and add pepper, salt, garlic powder, minced garlic, minced onion, sesame seeds, and onion powder.
  3. When ramen is cooked, add minimal pasta water to dissolve seasoning in bowl, and drain pasta. Add pasta to bowl.
  4. Top ramen with dumplings and enjoy.
